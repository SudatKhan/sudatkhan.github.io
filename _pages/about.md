I'm an astrophysics Ph.D. student at the University of Nevada, Las Vegas and am part of the [Star and Planet Formation Group](https://unlv-spfg.github.io). Working with my advisor, Dr. Zhaohuan Zhu, I focus on computational astrophysical fluid dynamics/radiation hydrodynamics, planet formation, circumplanetary disks, and protoplanetary disks/planet-disk interactions. I am also interested in N-Body simulations and planetary dynamics.

 Born and raised in NYC, I received my B.Sc. in 2024 from Stony Brook University in Physics and Astronomy. While pursuing my B.Sc., I worked with Dr. Philip J. Armitage on hydrodynamics simulations of planetary mergers and Dr. Wenrui Xu on planet-disk interactions which cemented my interests in computational astrophysics and planets.
